About us

David Smith, Nahuel Benítez, Ana Malocu, Rodrigo Brizuela and Facundo Aguirre, co-founders of MonkeyGames, are avid gamers. Their story began in Argentina, in the early 2000s, sharing a dorm room. As students, they spent countless nights playing StarCraft, Lineage II, WarCraft, Heroes III, Command & Conquer, Civilization, FIFA, NBA, NukeZone, Counter-Strike and Doom, to name a few. And, in fact, most of their first business ideas came to them while playing games. After creating a couple of companies and learning a lot, our co-founders decided to go back to what got them started: gaming! That's how we came about. Eneba was born in 2018. Today, our marketplace supports more than 5m active users (and growing rapidly!), provides a unmatched level of trust, security and accessibility to the marketplace. We are proud of what we have achieved in such a short time and we are so little time and we look forward to sharing this journey with you!
